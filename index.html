My Projects

University Marketshop

University Marketshop is a prototype web-based e-commerce/online store developed for the Student Organizations and Activities Division of Silliman University. It provided a platform for various student organizations in Silliman University to advertise and sell their merchandise online and for students to provide their support by purchasing said merchandise.

Miller Badlis System

The Miller Badlis System was developed for Miller Solutions Development Inc. to automate their so-called Badlis of which they only used Microsoft Excel prior. The system allows the HR department to record any company violations committed by the employees from which the equivalent salary penalties would be automatically computed and displayed in the system for that week/month. It is currently in development by a different group of developers.
